OUR COURSES

We offer a variety of courses to equip students with the skills they need for a successful career.

Electrical & Electronic Engineering Technology

Train students to qualify as craftsman in the Electrical Engineering industry, equipped to work in construction, maintenance, and distribution of electrical systems.

Information Communication Technology

Offering training in computer hardware, software, networking, website design, graphic design, video editing, and computer servicing for a complete tech skillset.

Auto Mechanical Engineering Technology

Acquire skills in accurate measurement, gear cutting, and thread cutting, with hands-on training in machine tools and technical instruments.

Mechanical Engineering Technology

Training in machining and maintenance fitting with practical exposure to using tools like lathe, drilling, and milling machines for mechanical engineering applications.

Oil and Gas Technology

Comprehensive training in the oil and gas industry lifecycle, equipping students with the necessary skills for efficient project delivery and leadership.

Refrigeration & Air-Conditioning Technology

Students learn to install, design, operate, and repair various cooling systems with knowledge and skills that meet industry standards.
